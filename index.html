Sonic Chaos Emerald Challenge

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are a set of Chaos Emeralds displayed below. By clicking on an emerald, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You objective is to win the game by matching the total score to the random numbers provided in each crystal. You will however lose the game if your total score goes above the random number given to you.

The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new game begins, the value of each crystal will change.

Good luck to you and have fun.
